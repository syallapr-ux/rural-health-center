Safe Motherhood Initiative

Providing comprehensive pre-natal and post-natal care for every rural family.

Our Maternity Roadmap

First Trimester (0-12 Weeks)

Focus on early registration, blood tests, and ultrasound to confirm healthy development. Free folic acid and iron supplements are provided to all registered patients.

Second Trimester (13-26 Weeks)

Regular monitoring of blood pressure and fetal movements. Anomaly scans and specialized nutritional counseling are conducted by our resident gynecologists.

Third Trimester & Post-Partum

Delivery planning and 24/7 ambulance standby. Our post-partum program includes lactation support and infant immunization tracking.
