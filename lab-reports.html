How to Read Your Verified Reports

Hemoglobin (Hb)

Male: 13.5-17.5 g/dL

Female: 12.0-15.5 g/dL

Source: ICMR Guidelines
Blood Glucose (Fasting)

Normal: 70–99 mg/dL

Prediabetes: 100–125 mg/dL

Source: WHO Diabetes Criteria
Blood Pressure

Normal: 120/80 mmHg

Hypertensive: Above 140/90

Source: AHA Standards
