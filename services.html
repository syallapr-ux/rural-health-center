Digital Health Services

Citizen-centric healthcare services delivered through a unified digital platform

Rural Health Connect provides an integrated digital interface for essential healthcare services mandated under IPHS 2022 and aligned with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM).

The platform is designed to support both citizen-facing services and health system operations, ensuring accessibility, transparency, and continuity of care.

Technical & Architectural Overview

Rural Health Connect is designed using a modular, API-first architecture. While this prototype uses simulated data, the system is capable of seamless integration with:

  • e-Hospital / HMIS systems
  • ABDM Gateway using FHIR resources
  • State Health Data Repositories

This design ensures scalability, interoperability, and long-term sustainability for government deployments.
