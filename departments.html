First Referral Unit (FRU) | IPHS 2022 Standards | District Health System

Facility Classification Overview

This Community Health Center is designated as a First Referral Unit (FRU) under the National Health Mission. It provides comprehensive secondary-level healthcare services including emergency obstetric care, trauma stabilization, and essential diagnostics.

  • 24×7 emergency and referral stabilization
  • Maternal & newborn care (EmONC)
  • National disease control programme integration
  • Referral linkage to District Hospital

Core Clinical Departments

Emergency & Casualty

24×7 trauma care and life-saving interventions.

  • Road traffic accident stabilization
  • Snake bite & poisoning management
  • Basic life support & triage
  • 108 ambulance coordination
Critical Services Active
Obstetrics & Gynecology

Comprehensive maternal healthcare services.

  • 24×7 delivery services
  • Emergency C-sections
  • ANC & PNC clinics
  • High-risk pregnancy referrals
EmONC Enabled
General Medicine

  • Clinical pathology & biochemistry lab

  • ECG & basic radiology

Referral & Escalation Protocol

Patients requiring tertiary care are stabilized and referred using government ambulance networks. Digital referral notes are prepared as per ABDM-FHIR simulation guidelines.

  • District Hospital referral linkage
  • Emergency transport coordination
  • Digital case summary generation
