Digitizing Rural Care

RH-Connect is a **Integrated Health Information System (IHIS)** designed to bridge the urban-rural healthcare divide. We focus on three core pillars of the **National Health Mission (NHM)**:

  • Information Retrieval: Providing real-time visibility into bed and medicine availability.
  • ABDM Interoperability: Preparing local clinics for the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission.
  • Offline Resilience: Utilizing Service Workers to ensure accessibility in low-connectivity zones.
