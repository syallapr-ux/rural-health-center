About Rural Health Connect

A Digital Public Health Enablement Platform for Rural India

Vision & Problem Statement

Rural Health Connect was conceptualized to address the information asymmetry, accessibility gaps, and service fragmentation that exist in rural healthcare delivery systems.

In many rural regions, patients travel long distances without knowing whether essential services such as beds, blood units, diagnostics, or specialists are available. This platform digitizes that information layer to improve decision-making for patients, health workers, and administrators.

Core Objectives

  • Provide real-time visibility of facility readiness
  • Enable rural-first digital access to healthcare information
  • Simulate ABDM-compliant digital health workflows
  • Improve referral efficiency and emergency response
  • Demonstrate offline-capable healthcare portals

System Architecture Overview

Rural Health Connect follows a client-side progressive web architecture, optimized for low-bandwidth rural environments.

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript
  • Data Layer: JSON-based facility datasets
  • Offline Support: Service Workers & Cache API
  • Security: HTTPS deployment (GitHub Pages)
