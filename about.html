About Rural Health Connect

Rural Health Connect is a prototype **Integrated Health Information System** designed to strengthen rural healthcare delivery under the **National Health Mission (NHM)**.

  • Real-time facility & inventory visibility
  • ABDM-ready digital health records
  • Offline-first architecture for rural areas
