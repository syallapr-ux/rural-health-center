Vaccine Safety & Guidelines

All vaccines provided at our Rural Centers are WHO-certified and maintained under strict Cold Chain Management (stored between 2°C to 8°C). Our staff are trained to manage Adverse Events Following Immunization (AEFI).

What to bring?

Mother-Child Protection (MCP) card and Aadhar ID of the parent.

Post-Vaccine Care

Mild fever is normal. Apply cold compress at the injection site.
