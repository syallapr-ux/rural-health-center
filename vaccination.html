Mission Indradhanush

This facility participates in Mission Indradhanush to ensure 100% vaccination coverage for children in "gray areas" and hard-to-reach rural hamlets.

Cold Chain Integrity

All vaccines are stored between +2°C to +8°C. We use WHO-certified ILRs (Ice Lined Refrigerators) to maintain vaccine potency.
