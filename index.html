About Rural Health Connect

Rural Health Connect is a prototype Integrated Health Information System designed to digitally enable First Referral Units (FRUs), Primary Health Centres (PHCs), and Community Health Centres (CHCs).

The platform focuses on improving accessibility, transparency, and continuity of care for rural populations by providing a single digital interface for citizens, healthcare providers, and administrators.

  • Aligned with ABDM & National Digital Health Blueprint
  • Designed for low-bandwidth rural environments
  • Supports referral & escalation workflows
  • Modular and scalable architecture
